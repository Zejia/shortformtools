Contact

Questions, corrections, or partnership notes.

If you want to report a preset issue, suggest another market mode, ask about a tool improvement, or discuss partnership and advertising opportunities, contact the site owner at hello@crossborderkit.com.

For legal, broker, or tax advice, contact a qualified customs specialist in the destination market. This site cannot provide filing advice. For page corrections, include the page URL and the assumption you think should change.

Correction requests

Send the page URL, the calculator input or preset you are questioning, the market involved, and the source or broker note that suggests the assumption should change. This makes it easier to distinguish a typo from a market-specific rule issue.

Tool suggestions

Good suggestions describe one repeatable import-planning task, the inputs an operator already has, and the output they need before a quote, supplier decision, or broker handoff.

Partnership and advertising

Use the same email for sponsorship, advertising, or tool partnership inquiries. Commercial placements should fit import operations, ecommerce logistics, customs preparation, sourcing, or landed-cost planning.

What not to send

Do not send confidential shipment documents, personal identifiers, payment details, or legally sensitive customs files. This site is a public planning resource, not a secure broker portal.
