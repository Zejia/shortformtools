Threshold notes

    Use this page for

    • Checking whether a shipment just under or over the threshold changes the commercial outcome.
    • Stress-testing whether tax still applies even when duty relief is modeled.
    • Comparing how much per-unit landed cost moves before you build price or channel strategy around a threshold assumption.

    What this is not

    Threshold logic is not universal.

    Duty relief is not always tax relief

    Many teams flatten the two together. This tool keeps them separate because some markets still collect VAT or other tax even when duty is reduced or waived.

    Collection method still matters

    Threshold treatment can also change depending on whether the shipment is broker-cleared, marketplace-collected, or handled through a special program.

    Do not price from hope

    If the deal only works when relief is applied, confirm the collection path with your broker or importer-of-record setup before you ship.
