About

Import planning should feel operational, not ornamental.

Import Duty Toolkit is a static planning site built for teams that need a sharper first-pass landed-cost view before they talk to a broker, quote a buyer, or place a reorder.

Reviewed . This site is built as a planning aid, not a government, legal, tax, or customs-advisory service.

What the site does

It helps operators combine product value, freight, insurance, duty, tax, brokerage, and misc overhead into a landed-cost view that is easier to price from.

What the site does not do

It does not replace customs brokers, legal review, classification advice, country-specific tax registrations, or filing instructions from your importer of record.

Why the pages are tool-led

Because shallow SEO pages do not help anyone. Each page is meant to solve a real planning job, whether that is rate estimation, landed-cost quoting, or HS-code preparation.
