Notes

Instagram can collapse blank lines in captions. This formatter adds invisible separators between paragraphs so spacing survives after pasting.

When to use line breaks

Use line breaks to separate the hook, story, call to action, and hashtags. Avoid adding too many gaps because long captions can hide the call to action below the fold.

Can I use this for Instagram bios?

Yes. Keep the bio short and preview it after pasting because profile layouts vary by device.

Does it add hashtags?

No. It only formats spacing, so the caption remains under your control.
