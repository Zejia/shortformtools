Hashtag strategy

Use hashtags to clarify the topic, not to replace a good hook. A small set of relevant tags is usually cleaner than a long block of unrelated trending tags.

How many TikTok hashtags should I use?

There is no fixed number. Start with 5-10 relevant tags and watch whether reach, saves, and completion rate improve.

Should I always use #fyp?

You can test it, but topic clarity matters more. Pair broad tags with niche-specific tags that explain who the video is for.
