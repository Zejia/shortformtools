What the score checks

The score favors titles that are long enough to be specific, short enough to scan, and clear about the target topic near the beginning.

Practical title pattern

Put the core topic first, then add the angle. For example: “TikTok Engagement Rate Calculator: Measure Any Video” is easier to scan than a vague curiosity headline.

Is 70 characters a hard limit?

No. It is a practical scanning target. YouTube can show longer titles, but important words may be truncated on smaller screens.

Should every title include the exact keyword?

Not always. Use the exact keyword when it reads naturally. Clarity and click intent matter more than forcing a phrase.
