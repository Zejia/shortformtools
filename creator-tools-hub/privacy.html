Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Creator Tools Hub may collect and how it may be used. Last updated: 2026-06-06.

Information we collect

Our tools run in your browser. Values you type into calculators and formatters are processed locally unless a future feature clearly states otherwise.

Do not enter confidential campaign contracts, private client data, passwords, payment information, or other sensitive personal details into public creator tools. The calculators and formatters are designed for ordinary planning examples, public metrics, draft text, and lightweight workflow checks.

Analytics and advertising

We may use analytics tools to understand page visits and performance. We may also display ads through advertising partners such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies or similar technologies.

Advertising partners may use cookies, device information, page activity, and similar signals to measure ad performance, prevent abuse, limit repeat impressions, and personalize ads where consent and local rules allow. External providers operate under their own privacy policies.

Cookies

Cookies may be used for analytics, advertising, security, and remembering basic preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Email and corrections

If you contact the site by email, the message may be retained to respond to your request, fix a page, review a tool suggestion, handle advertising or partnership questions, or prevent abuse. You may request correction or deletion of email correspondence unless retention is needed for security, accounting, legal, or business records.

Tool output and local storage

Some pages may use browser storage to remember a preference or make copy/download workflows smoother. Clearing browser storage or using a private browsing session may remove those preferences. The core public tools should remain usable without creating an account.
