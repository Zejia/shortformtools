Why creators use this site

Most pages here solve one narrow workflow problem. We keep the inputs public, show the formulas in plain English, and link related tools together so creators can move from checking a metric to fixing the underlying packaging or copy.

Clear formulas

Each calculator is built around visible assumptions instead of black-box scoring. That makes outputs easier to trust and compare across platforms.

Lightweight pages
