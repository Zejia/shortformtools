How to use it

Enter a view count and a low/high RPM range. If you do not know your RPM, test a conservative range first, then update it when you have platform or sponsor data.

Is this an official TikTok payout calculator?

No. It estimates scenarios for planning. Actual payouts depend on TikTok programs, region, video eligibility, ads, affiliate revenue, sponsorships, and product sales.

Why include engagement rate?

Higher engagement often improves distribution and sponsor value. The multiplier is intentionally modest so the estimate does not become fantasy math.
