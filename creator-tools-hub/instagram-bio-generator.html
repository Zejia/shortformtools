    Bio checklist

    A useful Instagram bio usually names the audience, describes the outcome, and gives one next step. Avoid stuffing it with broad buzzwords that could fit any account.

    What should an Instagram bio include?

    Use one clear audience, one promise, and one credibility signal or call to action. Keep it readable before adding emojis or line breaks.

    Can I use this for a business account?

    Yes. Replace the niche with your customer type and the promise with the result your product or service helps them get.
