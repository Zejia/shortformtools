    TikTok bio notes

    A TikTok bio has to work quickly. Name the audience, give a reason to follow, and avoid vague phrases like daily content or good vibes unless they are part of a specific brand voice.

    How long should a TikTok bio be?

    Shorter is usually easier to scan. Start with one sentence, then add proof or a call to action only if it makes the account clearer.

    Should I mention my niche?

    Yes, if it helps the right viewer understand the account. A focused niche also supports stronger video hooks and hashtags.
