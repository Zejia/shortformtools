Formula

Engagement rate = (likes + comments + shares + saves) ÷ followers or views × 100. Use the same denominator when comparing multiple accounts.

Follower rate vs view rate

Follower-based engagement is useful for account audits and influencer comparisons. View-based engagement is better for individual videos because TikTok distribution can reach far beyond existing followers.

What is a good TikTok engagement rate?

For many accounts, 2-4% is workable, 4-8% is healthy, and 8%+ is strong. Benchmarks vary by niche, audience size, and whether you divide by followers or views.

Why include saves?

Saves signal intent and future value. If you do not have save data, leave it at zero and compare results consistently.
