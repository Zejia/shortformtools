How to read the result

The best title is rarely the one with the highest keyword density. This tester rewards a balanced title: clear enough to understand, specific enough to promise value, and compact enough to survive mobile truncation.

Should I always choose the highest score?

Use the score as a tie-breaker, not a command. If the winner does not match the thumbnail or video payoff, fix the package before publishing.

Why does curiosity matter?

Curiosity gives a viewer a reason to click, but it only works when the topic and payoff stay clear. Vague curiosity titles usually create weak retention.
