    When bundle pricing helps

    Bundles can raise average order value, but only if the savings story and cost structure both work.

    Gift sets

    Show enough buyer savings to feel like a set, while checking that extra packaging and shipping do not erase the margin.

    Digital bundles

    Bundle templates, printables, or downloads by tracking the small processing fees and the real time spent creating support material.

    Ad-safe offers

    Stress-test 12% or 15% Offsite Ads before turning a bundle into the main promoted listing.
