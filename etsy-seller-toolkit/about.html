About Etsy Seller Toolkit

This site helps Etsy sellers pressure-test prices before a listing, discount, shipping change or ad sale. The calculators use publicly available Etsy fee references and make assumptions visible so sellers can adjust them.

The project is maintained as an independent publisher-style tool site. It may use ads or analytics to support upkeep, but the goal is still straightforward: help sellers make better pricing decisions with transparent math.
