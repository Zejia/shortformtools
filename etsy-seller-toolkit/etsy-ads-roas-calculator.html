    How to use Etsy Ads ROAS

    ROAS is only useful when it is tied to product margin and conversion rate.

    Break-even ROAS

    The lowest ROAS that avoids losing money. If your listing needs a 4x break-even ROAS, a 2x campaign is not really working even if it brings sales.

    Max CPC

    The highest click cost your conversion rate can support. Better photos, reviews and listing clarity can raise this number by improving conversion.

    Target ROAS

    A safer benchmark that keeps profit after ads. Use it as the number to beat before raising budget.

    Is this the same as the Offsite Ads calculator?

    No. Etsy Ads is usually CPC-based, so this calculator starts from clicks and conversion rate. Offsite Ads are attributed order fees, which are better modeled as a percentage of the order.

    What conversion rate should I enter?

    Use your listing or shop conversion rate if you know it. If not, test several scenarios such as 1.5%, 3%, and 5% to see how fragile the ad math is.

    When should I increase Etsy Ads budget?

    Raise budget only when the campaign clears target ROAS and still leaves acceptable profit per order. If it only clears break-even, improve the listing or pricing first.
