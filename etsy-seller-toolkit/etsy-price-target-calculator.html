    When to use this instead of a normal fee calculator

    This page is for price design: deciding what the listing should be before you publish, discount, bundle, or advertise it.

    Before a new listing

    Start with cost, labor, packaging, shipping, and target margin so you do not anchor on a competitor's underpriced listing.

    Before a coupon

    Enter the discount you want to survive, then solve the item price that still leaves your target profit intact.

    Before Offsite Ads

    Stress-test 12% or 15% attributed orders so one promoted sale does not quietly erase the whole margin.
