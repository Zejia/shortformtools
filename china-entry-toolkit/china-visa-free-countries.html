Which countries can currently enter China visa-free?

This page lists the countries on China's current unilateral 30-day visa-free policy as modeled in this release. It also shows whether each country is on the 240-hour transit without visa list.

Current public list

How to use this page

Use the region filters to narrow the list, then jump into the main checker if you want to test a trip purpose and stay length, not just nationality coverage.

What this list means

Being on the unilateral list does not mean every trip is automatically covered. The traveler still needs the right trip purpose, an ordinary passport, and a stay of up to 30 days under the current public policy model.

What this list does not mean

If a country is not shown here, that does not automatically mean the traveler cannot enter China without a visa in every case. Mutual exemption agreements, regional visa-free programs, and transit rules can create separate paths.
