# China Entry Toolkit

register: product

China Entry Toolkit is a public decision-support site for foreigners planning a trip to China.

Users arrive with a high-stakes question, not a vague curiosity:

- Can I enter China without a visa?
- Does my route qualify for 240-hour transit?
- How long can I stay?
- Which rule applies to my passport and trip?

The product should feel calm, precise, and operational. It should feel closer to a travel control desk than a blog or affiliate roundup.

Tone:

- direct
- grounded
- practical
- lightly reassuring

Anti-references:

- generic tourism landing pages
- fake embassy styling
- dark SaaS dashboards
- article-first layouts that hide the tool
- purple-on-white AI templates
