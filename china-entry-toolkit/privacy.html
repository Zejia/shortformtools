You can decline ad cookies and continue using the tools. This policy may change as the site grows and should be read together with the privacy terms of any active analytics or advertising providers used on the live site.

Your choices

You can use browser settings to block or clear cookies and local storage. You may also request correction or deletion of email correspondence by contacting the site owner, subject to security, abuse-prevention, legal, accounting, or legitimate business retention needs.
