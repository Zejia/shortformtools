Timing reminder

Both policy families referenced in this release use a stay clock that starts from 00:00 on the day after entry. That is why a date-and-time calculator is useful even when you already know the headline limit.

When to use this page

Use it when you already know the nationality rule but need to understand the timing of your actual itinerary. It is especially helpful when a route is close to the edge of the 240-hour transit window.

What this page does not do

This page does not decide nationality coverage, trip-purpose coverage, or whether a route satisfies the third-country or third-region rule. Use the other tools for those judgments.
