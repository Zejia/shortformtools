# Design Direction

Scene:

A traveler in a bright airport lounge is checking China entry rules on a laptop while juggling flights, passports, and hotel bookings. The interface should feel fast to scan, trustworthy under stress, and polished enough to use on desktop or phone without second-guessing.

Theme:

- Light
- Warm paper background
- Deep slate and marine ink
- Brass and persimmon accents for emphasis

Typography:

- Headings: serif with editorial authority
- Body: clean sans for quick scanning

Layout notes:

- Tool first
- Supporting explanation below the fold
- Split layouts over repetitive card grids
- Dense data should feel like a control panel, not a marketing page

Motion:

- short fades and panel lifts
- no playful bounce
