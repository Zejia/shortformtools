For checker issues, include the selected passport nationality, purpose, route, stay window, port or area choice, and the result you expected. This helps separate a data issue from a traveler-specific exception that should be verified with official authorities.

Partnership and advertising

Commercial inquiries should fit the site audience: travelers, relocation teams, travel operators, cross-border payment providers, local transport services, or practical arrival support. Advertising does not influence eligibility explanations or official-source review notes.

What not to send

Do not send passport scans, visa files, booking confirmations, payment information, medical details, or other sensitive personal records. China Entry Toolkit is a public information site, not a secure immigration case portal.
